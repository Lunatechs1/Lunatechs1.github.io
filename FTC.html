What Is FTC?

Students in the FIRST Tech Challenge develop their engineering thinking. Teams create, assemble, and program robots to take against rival teams in an alliance format. Robots can be programmed using a range of Java programming levels and are constructed utilizing a reusable platform that is driven by Android technology.
