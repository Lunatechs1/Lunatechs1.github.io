About Luna Techs

The Luna Techs are a passionate group of young innovators based in South Technical High School, our team is made of individuals who share a common goal: to push the boundaries of technology through collaboration and creativity. Each year, we come together to design and build robots for competitions like the FIRST Robotics Competition, where we apply our skills in engineering, programming, and problem-solving. Beyond the competitions, we focus on fostering teamwork, developing leadership skills, and inspiring each other to think outside the box. We start every season striving to be better than the last. We are accomplished in multiple categories, we are competition finalists as well as winners of the design award. We as a team reinvent ourselves and challenge our own designs every day. At Luna Techs, we're productive with all teammates having their own special roles; everybody contributes to our success.

Awards

Our team has won a multitude of accolades, including 2nd place at the August 31st Robot Rumble @ WWT RACEWAY and the Design Award from our 2023-24 FTC Robotics season. Luna Techs Robotics has garnered recognition for its innovative designs and cutting-edge engineering. As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to pushing boundaries and challenging ourselves. While also having a ton of fun!
